PArt Charity Auction
15. 06. –
01.07.2020
online auction by VAN HAM
initiated by Spiegelberger foundation
The PArt initiative supports artists who are affected by the consequences of the corona pandemic caused by exhibition cancellations and massive sales declines.

Centerpiece of the initiative is an online charity auction conducted by the auction house VAN HAM.
In this auction works by renowned artists such as Henrik Eiben, Frank Gerritz, Anselm Reyle, Michael Sailstorfer, Michael Wesely or Christian Awe are auctioned to support the initiative.

The net sales amount will go in equal parts to the consignor and to the PArt Emergency Aid Fund to benefit artists affected by the crisis.

26.06. –

Eschborn/Frankfurt next
to the German Stock Exchange
Art in public space project in cooperation with ImmoMa

Building M55, Mergenthalerallee 55, 65760 Eschborn
Preview and presentation of the new murals:
Get-together on Friday, June 26 5pm - 9pm
Art brunch on Saturday, June 27 12 noon - 4pm

All over the world, museums and galleries are closed, but I’m all the more creative. To reduce the deprivation of culture and to send a powerful signal of joie de vivre and optimism into the world, I’m creating new murals in Eschborn/Frankfurt, right next to the German Stock Exchange.

Four murals - with a height of 40 meters each, freely accessible, safe, free of charge, perceptible. And again with a focus on the good cause of my school and hospital building in Burkina Faso. Creativity won’t stop.
